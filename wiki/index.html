Outlawcraft Wiki

Outlawcraft is a private SMP server inspired by the well known YouTuber server, Hermitcraft. Having started as a band of 6 players when we first started in Season 1 in 2018 to the 100% full 45 player server we are now, we truly have been on a long, magnificant and blocky journey.

The Blocky History of Outlawcraft

The First Season

The First season was a blast, but a small one, the first Outlawcraft ender dragon was slain by ChrDaiJon and H26 (Hendobrine26) posted two videos on the server, one when the server was restarted due to a hacker, sanguisbebimus, and another about his first experiences. To date these are the only public videos of Outlawcraft on Yotube, but with H26 rerstarting his channel, there may be more.

The other players who took part in the begging of this amazing server were samsausage, LeoLion66, XxHypremixX (Now Chiitra) and woodenmonkey. Points of interest were Woody's wood shop, PETL's super smelter and the eyesore of PETL's Iron farm that the players saw everyday.

Unfortunately the server became inactive a few months in and it would take some time until Season 2 began.
