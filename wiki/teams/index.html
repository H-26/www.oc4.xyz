Teams/Alliances

You know why people play on servers? To play with people! That's why we have this page which is a list of the teams and alliances!

OGs

The OGs are a group of older players on the server who have been playing for at least a year. They do things to keep the server active but sometimes at the expense of newer players. Some of the members are also pretty inactive. They include: Teaah, ChrDaiJon, H26, iS3, NutbarWedge, LeoLion66, mous, samsausage, Tuffers213, Vortex, woodenmonkey and Chiitra.

Silly Clan

Once planning to be the most powerful group on the sever, threatening war with moustopia, they now are a peaceful, but huge team who live in a large village to the north of moustopia. Their leader, Sillyman1238210, has denied that any preperations are being made for war, but those on the outside don't know for sure. This proud clan was founded on the 2nd of November by Sillyman1238210 and ValveCorp. Currently, the co-owner is ceo_ofchill as ValveCorp left the silly clan for Moustopia for an unknown reason. Their current members are eteaxup, goblinhat098, Z4DanieL (inactive, Nightavenger375, weepingwillow111, LexiCat7676, Benxxy and UneducatedSwine. Sillyland (Their base) can be found at 500 -1000. The land has a pumpkin farm, wheat farm, carrot farm, melon farm, auto pumpkin farm, beetroot farm, cocoa farm, Chicken farm, cow farm, sheep farm, potato farm, sugar cane farm, auto sugar cane farm and tree farm. You can also find an artificial village where they keep their villagers, and many buildings created by their members.
